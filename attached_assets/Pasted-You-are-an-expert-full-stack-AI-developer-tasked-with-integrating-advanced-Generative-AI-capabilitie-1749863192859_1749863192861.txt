You are an expert full-stack AI developer tasked with integrating advanced Generative AI capabilities into a healthcare admin platform focused on Quality Assurance and Performance Improvement (QAPI), Risk Management, and Compliance Automation.

Your goal is to build a real-time, AI-powered system that validates and confirms the accuracy of all input and tracked information to ensure admins have reliable, actionable insights into quality and compliance performance.

Feature Requirements:
QAPI Dashboard

Develop a dashboard that tracks and visualizes key quality indicators such as:

Patient falls

Infections

Wounds

Missed visits

Readmissions

Provide dynamic trendlines for both patients and clinicians over customizable time frames.

Automatically flag Centers of Medicare & Medicaid Services Conditions of Participation (CoP) or survey risk areas based on thresholds and historical data.

Use Generative AI to cross-check incoming data for consistency and anomalies to confirm correctness before dashboard updates.

Automated QAPI Reporting

Generate monthly QAPI summary reports featuring:

Graphs visualizing trends and performance

A summary of top issues identified

AI-generated root cause analysis suggestions for each issue

Allow export of reports in formats suitable for surveyors or internal quality meetings.

Use Gen AI to validate that reported data matches the underlying raw data, ensuring no discrepancies.

Performance Improvement Projects (PIPs) Assistant

Implement an AI assistant that:

Recommends PIPs tailored to agency-specific quality or compliance challenges detected.

Assigns tasks automatically to responsible staff members.

Tracks progress with deadline alerts and escalation notifications.

Confirm that task assignments and statuses are correct using AI-driven data verification.

Staff Compliance Monitor

Track compliance metrics including:

Documentation timeliness

Credential expirations

Training logs and completions

Notify HR or admins proactively about overdue or upcoming compliance requirements.

Use Gen AI to validate the accuracy and timeliness of compliance data and alerts.

Additional Requirements:
All features must support real-time data updating with automated verification by Gen AI to reduce human error.

Design user-friendly UI/UX that provides clear visibility and actionable insights.

Provide audit logs for all AI confirmations and flagged inconsistencies.

Ensure HIPAA compliance and data security best practices throughout.
